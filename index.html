Experiences

Embrace the Great Outdoors

Immerse yourself in curated outdoor activities designed to let you experience the best of Wayanad's landscapes.

🚣

Reservoir Boating

Hop onto a traditional boat or modern kayak and glide over the calm, glassy waters of the Karapuzha Reservoir during sunset.

🎣

Angling & Fishing

Find a quiet spot along our private waterfront bank and try your hand at catch-and-release fishing.

🔥

Lakeside Campfire

Gather around a warm crackling campfire at dusk, share stories, stargaze, and enjoy delicious barbecue grilled fresh for you.

🥾

Nature Trails

Guided walks through coffee and spice plantations, learning about Wayanad's rich biodiversity and farming culture.

🦜

Bird Watching

Spot beautiful native and migratory birds like kingfishers, egrets, and hornbills that nest around the reservoir shores.

🚴

Village Cycling

Pedal along scenic, mist-covered local village roads, meeting friendly locals and enjoying beautiful rural landscapes.

Specifications

Resort Details & Guidelines

Click on any specification below to learn more about our villa layouts, amenities, and policies.

Villa Space & Layout →
Our villas offer 1,200 sq. ft. of luxury space, comprising two fully furnished master bedrooms with attached bathrooms, a shared living lounge, a private kitchenette, and a waterfront veranda opening to private gardens.
Check-in & Check-out Policies →
Standard check-in is at 1:00 PM and check-out is at 11:00 AM. Early check-in or late check-out is subject to availability and prior confirmation.
Connectivity & Remote Work →
Each villa is equipped with dedicated high-speed Wi-Fi (up to 100 Mbps) backed by a robust generator power system, making it the perfect destination for working remotely in nature.
Occupancy & Extra Bedding →
Each villa comfortably accommodates up to 4 adults. Up to two extra rollaway beds can be provided upon request for children or additional guests (charges apply).
